Landing Page Design
I have created about 20 different landing pages. There is a function to display or hide the sub menu when the navigation menu is hovered by mouse. In addition, the photos on the main screen are implemented to operate in a slide format. I used Adobe Photoshop to edit the images to my needs. I also implemented a function to display a pop-up window when the title of the notice is clicked. Using the tab function, various menus such as notices and galleries are displayed or hidden depending on the click.
