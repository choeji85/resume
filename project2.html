You Did It! (Schedule Management Program)
What sets this application apart from other schedule management programs is the ability to display positive affirmations on the main screen of the program. Positive affirmations can be saved by the user and set to change automatically as a desktop image, or they can be imported like a library by looking at example phrases from other users. It not only checks what I have to do, but also shows whether I have accomplished it. The core of this project is to give the user a sense of accomplishment and satisfaction through this.
